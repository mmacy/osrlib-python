# Hold Portal

*1st Level [Magic-User Spell](/srd/index.php/Magic-User_Spells "Magic-User Spells")*

**Duration:** 2d6 turns

**Range:** 10’

Magically prevents a portal (e.g. door, gate, etc.) from being opened.

- **Opening by magic:** A *[knock](/srd/index.php/Knock "Knock")* spell opens the held portal instantly.
- **Opening by force:** Creatures with at least 3 Hit Dice more than the caster can open the held portal with one round of effort.
