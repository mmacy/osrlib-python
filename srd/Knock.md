# Knock

*2nd Level [Magic-User Spell](/srd/index.php/Magic-User_Spells "Magic-User Spells")*

**Duration:** 1 round

**Range:** 60’

Causes locked, barred, secured, or stuck doors, gates, chests, and so forth to open.

- **Magically held doors:** Are affected (e.g. *[hold portal](/srd/index.php/Hold_Portal "Hold Portal")*, *[wizard lock](/srd/index.php/Wizard_Lock "Wizard Lock")*).
- **Secret doors:** May be opened, but they must be known to the caster.
