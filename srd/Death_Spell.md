# Death Spell

*6th Level [Magic-User Spell](/srd/index.php/Magic-User_Spells "Magic-User Spells")*

**Duration:** Instant

**Range:** 240’

Up to 4d8 Hit Dice of creatures within a 60’ cube area must **save versus death** or die, instantly.

**Restrictions:** [Undead](/srd/index.php/Category:Undead "Category:Undead") and creatures with greater than 7 Hit Dice are unaffected.
