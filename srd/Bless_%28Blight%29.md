# Bless (Blight)

*2nd Level [Cleric Spell](/srd/index.php/Cleric_Spells "Cleric Spells")*

**Duration:** 6 turns

**Range:** 60’

May be used in one of two situations:

1. **Battle:** Allies within a 20’ square area who are not yet in melee gain a +1 bonus to attack and damage rolls and a +1 bonus to [morale](/srd/index.php/Morale_(Optional_Rule) "Morale (Optional Rule)").
2. **Ritual:** Casting *bless* may also form part of rituals of purification or consecration, at the [referee’s](/srd/index.php/The_Referee%E2%80%99s_Role "The Referee’s Role") discretion.


## Reversed: Blight

Incurs a -1 penalty to morale checks, attack rolls, and damage rolls of enemies within a 20’ square area. A **save versus spells** is allowed to resist the *blight*.
