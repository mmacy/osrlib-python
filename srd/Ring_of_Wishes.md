# Ring of Wishes

Grants the wearer’s wishes (see ***[Wishes](/srd/index.php/Magic_Items_(General)#Wishes "Magic Items (General)#Wishes")***).

- **Charges:** The number of wishes granted is noted in the ***[Rings](/srd/index.php/Rings "Rings")*** table. Once all wishes have been used, the ring’s magic is spent.
