# Wight

Corpses of humans or demihumans, possessed by malevolent spirits.

---

| Armour Class | 5 [14] |
| --- | --- |
| Hit Dice | 3* (13hp) |
| Attacks | 1 × touch (energy drain) |
| THAC0 | 17 [+2] |
| Movement | 90’ (30’) |
| Saving Throws | D12 W13 P14 B15 S16 (3) |
| Morale | 12 |
| Alignment | Chaotic |
| XP | 50 |
| Number Appearing | 1d6 (1d8) |
| Treasure Type | B |

---


- **Undead:** Make no noise, until they attack. Immune to effects that affect living creatures (e.g. poison). Immune to mind-affecting or mind-reading spells (e.g. *[charm](/srd/index.php/Charm "Charm")*, *[hold](/srd/index.php/Hold "Hold")*, *[sleep](/srd/index.php/Sleep "Sleep")*).
- **Mundane damage immunity:** Only harmed by [silver weapons](/srd/index.php/Other_Combat_Issues#Invulnerabilities_.28Optional_Rule.29 "Other Combat Issues") or magic.
- **Energy drain:** A successfully hit target permanently loses one experience level (or Hit Die). This incurs a loss of one Hit Die of hit points, as well as all other benefits due to the drained level (e.g. [spells](/srd/index.php/Spells "Spells"), [saving throws](/srd/index.php/Saving_Throws "Saving Throws"), etc.). A character’s XP is reduced to halfway between the former and new levels. A person drained of all levels becomes a wight in 1d4 days, under the control of the wight that killed them.
