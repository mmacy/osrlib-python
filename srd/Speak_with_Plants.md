# Speak with Plants

*4th Level [Cleric Spell](/srd/index.php/Cleric_Spells "Cleric Spells")*

**Duration:** 3 turns

**Range:** 30’

This spell has two usages:

1. **Normal plants:** Communication with normal plants. The caster may ask for simple favours and plants may comply, if the request is within their comprehension and capabilities. For example, densely overgrown plants may clear a passageway through which the caster and party may travel.
2. **Monstrous plants:** Communication with plant-like or plant-based monsters.
