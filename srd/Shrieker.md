Giant, subterranean mushrooms that can creep around slowly.

- **Shriek:** Triggered by light (up to 60’ away) or movement (up to 30’ away). Shriek lasts for 1d3 rounds. Each round of the shriek, there is a 50% chance of a [wandering monster](/srd/index.php/Hazards_and_Challenges#Wandering_Monsters "Hazards and Challenges") being attracted (will arrive in 2d6 rounds).
