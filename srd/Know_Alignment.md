# Know Alignment

*2nd Level [Cleric Spell](/srd/index.php/Cleric_Spells "Cleric Spells")*

**Duration:** 1 round

**Range:** 10’

Reveals the [alignment](/srd/index.php/Alignment "Alignment") of one character, monster, object, or area within range. (Most objects or areas do not have an alignment, but [magic items](/srd/index.php/Magic_Items_(General) "Magic Items (General)") or holy places may.)
