# Remove Curse (Curse) (C)

*3rd Level [Cleric Spell](/srd/index.php/Cleric_Spells "Cleric Spells")*

**Duration:** Instant / Permanent (*curse*)

**Range:** The caster or a creature touched

This spell removes a single curse from the subject. It may allow a character to discard a cursed magic item.

## Reversed: Curse

Places a deleterious effect upon a creature, if it fails a **save versus spells**.

- **Effects:** The exact form and effects of the curse are determined by the caster.
- **Maximum possible effects include:** A –2 penalty to [saving throws](/srd/index.php/Saving_Throws "Saving Throws"), a –4 penalty to attack rolls, reducing an [ability score](/srd/index.php/Ability_Scores "Ability Scores") by 50%.
- **Multiple curses:** May afflict a creature, as long as each has a different effect.
- **Referee judgement:** The [referee](/srd/index.php/The_Referee%E2%80%99s_Role "The Referee’s Role") should judge the effects of this spell and may turn overly powerful curses back onto the caster!
