# Girdle of Giant Strength

A character who wears this girdle gains the strength of a [hill giant](/srd/index.php/Giant,_Hill "Giant, Hill").

- **Combat ability:** The wearer’s ability in combat is augmented: the character attacks as an 8 Hit Dice monster. (If the character already has better attack probabilities, the girdle does not alter these.)
- **Damage:** The wearer inflicts 2d8 damage in combat, or twice normal damage, if using the optional variable weapon damage rules (see ***[Combat](/srd/index.php/Combat "Combat")***).
