# Fighter



|  | |
| --- | --- |
| Requirements | None |
| Prime requisite | STR |
| Hit Dice | 1d8 |
| Maximum level | 14 |
| Armour | Any, including shields |
| Weapons | Any |
| Languages | Alignment, Common |



Fighters are adventurers trained in warfare and [combat](/srd/index.php/Combat "Combat"). In a group of adventurers, the role of fighters is to battle monsters and to defend other characters.

## Combat

Fighters can use all types of [weapons and armour](/srd/index.php/Weapons_And_Armour "Weapons And Armour").

## Stronghold

Any time a fighter wishes (and has sufficient money), they can [build](/srd/index.php/Construction "Construction") a [castle or stronghold](/srd/index.php/Structures "Structures") and [control the surrounding lands](/srd/index.php/Domain_Management "Domain Management").

## After Reaching 9th Level

A fighter may be granted a title such as Baron or Baroness. The land under the fighter’s control is then known as a Barony.
