# Potion of Giant Strength

Grants the strength of a [frost giant](/srd/index.php/Giant,_Frost "Giant, Frost").

- **Attacks with weapons:** Inflict double damage.
- **Throw boulders:** The character may throw boulders for 3d6 damage, with a range of up to 200’.
- **Non-cumulative:** The effects of this potion are not cumulative with other magic items that augment strength (e.g. a *[girdle of giant strength](/srd/index.php/Girdle_of_Giant_Strength "Girdle of Giant Strength")*).
