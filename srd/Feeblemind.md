# Feeblemind

*5th Level [Magic-User Spell](/srd/index.php/Magic-User_Spells "Magic-User Spells")*

**Duration:** Permanent

**Range:** 240’

An arcane spell caster (e.g. a [magic-user](/srd/index.php/Magic-User "Magic-User") or [elf](/srd/index.php/Elf "Elf")) within range must **save versus spells (at a -4 penalty)** or become an imbecile, unable to think clearly or cast [spells](/srd/index.php/Spells "Spells").
