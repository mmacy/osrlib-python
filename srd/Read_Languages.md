# Read Languages

*1st Level [Magic-User Spell](/srd/index.php/Magic-User_Spells "Magic-User Spells")*

**Duration:** 2 turns

**Range:** The caster

The caster gains the ability to understand written information in any [language](/srd/index.php/Languages "Languages"), including coded messages or symbols (e.g. on treasure maps). No ability to *speak* unknown languages is conferred.
