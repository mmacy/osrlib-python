# Sprite

1’ tall, winged humanoids, related to [pixies](/srd/index.php/Pixie "Pixie") and [elves](/srd/index.php/Elf_(Monster) "Elf (Monster)"). They are shy, but driven by curiosity and their odd sense of humour.

- **Curse:** Five sprites can collectively curse a target (no attack roll; **save versus spells**). The effect is determined by the [referee](/srd/index.php/The_Referee%E2%80%99s_Role "The Referee’s Role"), but will always be used to comical effect (e.g. target trips, target’s nose grows).
- **Pranksters:** Even if attacked, sprites have no interest in killing, merely in making practical jokes.
