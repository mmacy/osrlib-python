# Hold Person (MU)

*3rd Level [Magic-User Spell](/srd/index.php/Magic-User_Spells "Magic-User Spells")*

**Duration:** 1 turn per level

**Range:** 120’

This spell causes one or more humans, demihumans, or human-like monsters (*see **[Persons](/srd/index.php/General#Persons "General")***) to be paralysed if they fail a **saving throw versus spells**. It may be cast in two ways:

1. **Against an individual:** The target’s saving throw is penalised by -2.
2. **Against a group:** 1d4 individuals in the group are targeted.


**Restrictions:** [Undead](/srd/index.php/Category:Undead "Category:Undead") and human-like monsters of greater than 4+1 HD (see ***[Hit point modifiers](/srd/index.php/Game_Statistics_(Monsters)#Hit_Dice_.28HD.29 "Game Statistics (Monsters)")***) are not affected.
