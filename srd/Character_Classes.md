# Character Classes

A ***Class*** is an adventuring profession to which the character belongs. A character’s class defines their main abilities.

- **[Cleric](/srd/index.php/Cleric "Cleric"):** Clerics are human adventurers who have sworn to serve a deity. They are trained for battle and channel the power of their deity.
- **[Dwarf](/srd/index.php/Dwarf "Dwarf"):** Dwarves are stout, bearded demihumans, about 4’ tall and weighing about 150 pounds.
- **[Elf](/srd/index.php/Elf "Elf"):** Elves are slender, fey demihumans with pointed ears.
- **[Fighter](/srd/index.php/Fighter "Fighter"):** Fighters are human adventurers dedicated to mastering the arts of combat and war.
- **[Halfling](/srd/index.php/Halfling "Halfling"):** Halflings are small, rotund demihumans with furry feet and curly hair.
- **[Magic-User](/srd/index.php/Magic-User "Magic-User"):** Magic-users are human adventurers whose study of arcane secrets has taught them how to cast spells.
- **[Thief](/srd/index.php/Thief "Thief"):** Thieves are human adventurers who live by their skills of deception and stealth.
